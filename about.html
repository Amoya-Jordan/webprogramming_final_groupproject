About the Brand

Mission Statement

Auriva exists to bring pure, natural wellness into everyday life; offering thoughtfully sourced essential oils that nurture the body, calm the mind, and elevate the simplest moments.

Vision Statement

To become the most trusted name in natural wellness, inspiring people everywhere to make nature, healing power a part of their daily ritual.

Target Audience

Auriva speaks to three core groups: wellness and self-care enthusiasts seeking premium, responsibly sourced oils; everyday lifestyle shoppers looking to enhance their routines with natural products, and spa and salon owners requiring consistent, high-quality essential oils for their clients.
